OUR SERVICES Exhibition & Translation

From trade shows to trans-city roadshows, we work hard to ensure you have great presence at exhibitions, stand out from the crowd, and enhance business opportunities.

During the exhibitions, our well-trained exhibition assistant can act as both your interpreter and salesperson, and help you take care all the jobs, including introducing your products, recording all visitors' information and extracting potential customers.

After the event, our professional and experienced sales team will follow up all sales lead you acquired in the exhibition, as well as prepare and deliver product samples to potential customers. We can also accompany and assist you to visit your potential importers and distributors in China.

We can translate all your marketing materials and products information to Chinese, from your brochure and catalogue, to your official website and name cards. We work hard to break down culture barriers and make sure your stories can be understood by Chinese customers.

To successfully build your brand in China, we propose a bold and incisive market positioning to help you stand out. We contact with local decision-makers, target opinion leaders, key influencers, journalists and bloggers, and tell your brand stories to strengthen your credibility and make people take notice.

We ensure that we fully understand your company's core values, target audience and brand stories. All solutions we provide are made based on your objectives and requirements.
