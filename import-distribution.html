OUR SERVICES Import & Distribution

We are more than your importer and distributor. We work hard to become your long-term strategic business partner.

Holding import licenses issued by Chinese government, we have the right to handle your importation requirements. Our certified professional import experts work hard to give you the peace of mind when importing your products to China.

We are familiar with Chinese import regulations and obligations, customs declaration and China Inspection and Quarantine (CIQ) clearance.

We provide full range of services, including customs declaration and clearance, warehousing, logistics and delivery, as well as professional labeling and packaging, to ensure your products conforms to China's requirements.

With our advanced logistics system, all products are shipped and stored in excellent conditions to preserve their freshness and lifespan. Our air-conditioned vehicles and temperature-controlled warehouses guarantee the ideal environment of your products during the process of custom clearing, warehouse storage and products delivery.

Rather than focusing exclusively on one channel, we develop multi-channel distribution strategies to give you a mix of online and offline presence. According to your conditions and objectives, we can choose the most effective distribution channels for your products and brands.

Working with local distributors, wholesalers and sub-distributors, we bring together the best networks and expertise to help you sell more products to Chinese customers.
